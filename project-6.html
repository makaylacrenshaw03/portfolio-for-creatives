Creative Mornings Vancouver Portrait

Collaborating with Creative Mornings Vancouver, I had the exciting opportunity to create a portrait of guest speaker Uytae Lee—a columnist and filmmaker celebrated for his unique insights on Vancouver’s urban landscape. The portrait reflects key elements of his work, featuring a cityscape background, Uytae with his camera, and textures that evoke the essence of Vancouver's urban environment.
