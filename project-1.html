FAQsofLife (In Progress!)

The FAQsofLife project focuses on equipping young adults (ages 18-25) with essential skills for independent living. Many young adults find that school does not adequately prepare them for crucial topics such as financial management and household responsibilities, often leaving them to navigate these challenges on their own. This gap in resources can lead to increased reliance on parents and a lack of confidence in handling everyday tasks, such as building credit or paying bills. To address this issue, I designed a website and a social media campaign that provide practical tips and guidance on these vital skills, aiming to empower young adults and facilitate a smoother transition into adulthood.
